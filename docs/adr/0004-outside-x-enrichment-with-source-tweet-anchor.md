# Outside-X Enrichment With Source Tweet Anchor

The source tweet remains the anchor of every generation run, but the product may use outside-X enrichment as supporting context when the tweet and replies are too short or incomplete to recover the underlying news clearly. We chose this because the product's job is to produce stronger quote-tweet drafts about a specific source tweet, and that requires enough context to understand the news without letting external material replace the tweet as the center of the run.
